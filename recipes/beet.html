Recipe

Beetroot Smoothie

Description

A healthy, nutritious, and delicious beetroot smoothie that will power you throughout your day! Only takes about 10 minutes to prepare and consists of whole and fresh ingredients. Enjoy this drink anytime of the day!

Ingredients

  • Beetroot
  • Apple
  • Carrot
  • Spinach
  • Limes
  • Ice
  • Pom Juice
Steps
  1. Add pom juice to blender and add spinach, blend until smooth.
  2. Peel lime and throw into blender, cut apple and carrot, add to blender.
  3. Cut beetroot and add to blender, blend ingredients until smooth.
  4. Add ice and blend on high for 20 seconds, serve and enjoy.

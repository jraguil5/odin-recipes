Recipe

Iced Coffee

Description

A cold, bold, and refreshing coffee drink that will recharge your focus! This simple coffee drink is delicious and simple to make. It only requires 4 ingredients and 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Espresso
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Flavor
  • Ice
Steps
  1. Add milk, ice, and vanilla flavor to a shaker cup and shake for 2 minutes. Pour in a glass of your choice.
  2. Pull 2 shots of espresso from your preferred method and pour over the ice.
  3. Throw trendy straw into glass and enjoy!

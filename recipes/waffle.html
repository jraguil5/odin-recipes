Recipe

Waffle

Description

A fluffy, classic, belgian waffle that will amaze you for its ease to make. It only takes a couple of ingredients and can be prepared in less than 20 minutes. Impress your family and friends with this delicious classic belgian waffle!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Vanilla Bean and Vanilla Paste
Steps
  1. Separate the yolk from the egg and save the egg white.
  2. Melt butter, add milk, sugar, and egg yolk to container. Add butter to container and whisk
  3. Add vanilla bean and vanilla paste to container. Mix ingredients.
  4. Add flour, salt, and baking soda to the container. Do not over mix.
  5. Mix the egg whites until peaks are formed, add to main container and fold gently.
  6. Preheat a waffle iron and add batter to iron, let cook for 3 minutes or until steam dissipates.
  7. Serve waffle and enjoy!
